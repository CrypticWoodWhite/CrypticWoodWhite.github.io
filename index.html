About


I am a Full-Stack Web Developer with a certificate from the University of Washington, and with an MS in Marine Science and a BS in Environmental Sciences. I am analytically-minded and enjoy seeking out new information in order to solve specific problems. I am pssionate about learning for the sake of learning, which is why I originally went into science and am now in web development. And finally, while I am self-reliant, I very much appreciate hearing the unique perspectives that come out of working with a diverse team. I am determined to bring my 10 years of experience and drive in biological research to solving community and environmental problems through web development.

When I'm not thinking about code or biology, I enjoy all things outdoors, including hiking, backpacking, climbing, kayaking, fishing, diving, and skiing. I have an adventure cat who comes with me on many of these activities (although definitely not diving!). Additionally, I enjoy astrophotography, reading books about natural history and the history of polar exploration, cooking and baking for my friends, and a good microbrew or glass of cabernet at the end of a long day.
